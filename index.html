The Philosophy

This app won't
motivate you.

DRIVEN is not a productivity app. It is not a habit tracker. It does not send you encouraging messages or celebrate streaks.

It reflects what you do, asks you to be honest, and holds the standard you set. If you want comfort or encouragement, this isn't the app you're looking for.

If you're ready to take full responsibility for what you do each day — let's get to work.

01

Non-Negotiable

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Driven Systems LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates the DRIVEN mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use DRIVEN:

  • Account information: name, email address
  • Profile data: identity statement, personal why, limitless dream
  • Daily check-in data: entries you submit through Morning Setup, Midday Standard, and Death Journal
  • Usage data: check-in completion, streaks, and performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and operate the DRIVEN app
  • Generate your daily AI coaching pulse
  • Send weekly summaries to your designated coach (if enabled)
  • Calculate your resilience score and streak
  • Improve the app experience

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

AI Features

DRIVEN uses OpenAI's API to generate personalized coaching pulses based on your check-in data. Your data is processed in accordance with OpenAI's privacy policy.

Coach Sharing

If you choose to enable coach sharing, a weekly summary of your check-in data is sent via email to your designated coach. This feature is optional and can be disabled at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

DRIVEN is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact Us
