Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

DRIVEN ("we", "our", or "us") is a daily execution system for high performers. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not monetize your information. We use it to run the coaching system you signed up for.

What We Collect

When you use DRIVEN, we collect:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you create an account
  • Profile data — your identity statement, your why, and your limitless dream (things you write during onboarding)
  • Check-in responses — your answers during morning setup, midday reset, and daily exchange sessions
  • Voice transcripts — text transcriptions of your voice coaching sessions. Audio is not stored; only the text transcript.
  • Execution queue data — tasks, categories, and completion status you create in the app
  • Usage data — session timestamps, streak counts, notification preferences, and app settings
  • Device data — FCM push notification token for delivering reminders

How We Use Your Data

  • To power your AI coaching sessions — your context and history help Claude ask better questions and hold you accountable
  • To generate your daily coaching pulse — a personalized insight based on your check-in responses
  • To track your streak and progress over time
  • To send you push notifications at your chosen check-in times
  • To generate coach summaries if you have a human coach using DRIVEN
  • To improve the product — anonymized, aggregated usage patterns only

We never use your voice transcripts or check-in responses for advertising or share them with third parties for their own use.

Data Storage

  • Firebase / Google Cloud — your account, check-in data, execution queue, and pulse responses are stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud, us-central1 region)
  • Hetzner Cloud (Germany) — our voice coaching server processes real-time voice sessions. Transcripts are generated and forwarded to Firebase; raw audio is not retained.
  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256 via Firebase defaults)

Third-Party Services

DRIVEN uses the following services to deliver its functionality:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — powers the AI coaching conversations. Your messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Anthropic's privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy
  • OpenAI (Whisper) — used for voice transcription during voice sessions. Audio clips are sent to OpenAI's API and transcribed to text. OpenAI's privacy policy: openai.com/privacy
  • Resend — used to deliver transactional emails (waitlist confirmation, coach summaries). Resend's privacy policy: resend.com/privacy
  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and push notifications. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your account and associated data within 30 days. Check-in transcripts and coaching data are deleted as part of account deletion.

Your Rights

  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, email us at justin@driven.coach.

Children

DRIVEN is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will remove it promptly.

No Data Selling

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve DRIVEN.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated in the app. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
